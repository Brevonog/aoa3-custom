The mortal enemy of anyone with a fear of more than 6 legs on one creature. Muckopedes are generally only found in dry areas where they can use their natural colouring to blend into the ground.
如果有可能，它们喜欢在泥里洗澡，尽管这样似乎对这种动物没有实质性的好处。

Muckopedes are easily spooked and will attack out of fright if approached. Their bite applies a minor nerve agent that can cause drowsiness and mild weakness for a short time.
